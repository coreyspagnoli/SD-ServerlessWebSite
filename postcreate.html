Need help? – How to Create a Post

  • Please briefly describe material condition, quantity, and anything else you would like viewers to know.

  • Enter the units used to measure your quantity. For example, pounds, gallons, yards, etc.

  • If you select "Yes", we will create a post on Craigslist with your title, description, and a link to your post on Sleeping Donkey.

    EVERYTHING BELOW THIS IS OPTIONAL INFORMATION, BUT WE RECCOMEND COMPLETING THE FORM BECAUSE THE MORE INFO YOU GIVE, THE BETTER CHANCES YOU HAVE OF RECEIVING AN OFFER.

  • Select all industries you'd like to work with.

  • Briefly describe your experience (Ex. years of experience and previous work history)
  • Main Listing Image

    We reccomend using a square-shaped photo. Any photo will work, but your preview on this form will be cropped, and the full picture will display properly on your post. (Max File Size: 5MB). If you want to delete a photo, hover the cursor over the picture you want to delete.
  • Image Gallery

    You can add up to 6 additional photos here if you'd like. (If you want to delete a photo, hover the cursor over the picture you want to delete)
  • Select File(s)

    You can upload up to 3 additional files you'd like displayed on your post (Ex: resume, business plan, etc)
